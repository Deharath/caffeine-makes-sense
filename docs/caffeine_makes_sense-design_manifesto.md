# Caffeine Makes Sense – Design North Star

## Core Principle

Caffeine is a **masking agent**, not a fatigue cure.

It blocks the feeling of tiredness.
The underlying sleep pressure is still there.

As the caffeine fades, the fatigue that was already present becomes visible again.

---

## Design Goal

Caffeine should feel:

* Useful for pushing through dangerous hours
* Risky if relied on instead of sleeping
* Gradual in onset, not instant
* Consequential when mistimed, especially near sleep

The player should think:

"I can keep going, but I'll pay for this later."

Not:

"Free fatigue delete, time to chug."

---

## What Caffeine Should Affect

Caffeine should plug into existing wakefulness systems:

### 1. Fatigue Presentation

Caffeine should change what the player can get away with for a while, not erase true fatigue.

Effects:

* Displayed fatigue is projected downward while caffeine is active
* The strongest gameplay benefit lands in the meaningfully-tired band
* Severe fatigue is still only partially maskable

Result:
Coffee helps you push. It does not make you secretly well-rested.

---

### 2. Onset And Decay

Caffeine should behave like a curve, not a coupon.

Effects:

* Caffeine ramps in instead of applying instantly
* Item differences come mainly from dose and convenience, not fake separate chemistry
* Pills are the strongest deliberate push because they are the biggest dose
* Coffee and tea are milder because they are smaller doses
* All forms fade by half-life decay, not a hard timer

Result:
Timing matters. Planning matters. Late stimulant use has a tail.

---

### 3. Rebound

The downside of caffeine should come from unmasking, not a fake debuff.

Effects:

* Hidden fatigue accumulates underneath masking
* As caffeine fades, that fatigue naturally reappears
* There is no scripted crash penalty layered on top

Result:
The comedown feels borrowed, not arbitrarily inflicted.

---

### 4. Sleep Quality

Late caffeine should hurt the value of sleep more than the length of sleep.

Effects:

* Bedtime caffeine builds a disruption score across the sleep window
* Early sleep matters most, with weaker late-night disruption as caffeine decays
* Wake-up carries a bounded fatigue remainder based on that disruption
* Visible wake fatigue may still be partially masked if caffeine is still active

Result:
You slept, but the sleep was worse.

---

## What Caffeine Should NOT Do

Caffeine should not:

* Instantly remove fatigue in a flat chunk
* Restore true fatigue as if sleep debt disappeared
* Provide generic combat buffs or speed boosts
* Add new HUD clutter or special meters for ordinary play
* Make one normal coffee or pill feel cartoonishly punishing

---

## System Philosophy

No magic.
No free energy.
No fake crash curse.

Caffeine cost should emerge from:

* masking instead of restoration
* time-dependent onset and decay
* worse sleep when used badly

The game already has fatigue, sleep, moodles, and vision pressure.

CMS integrates into those systems. It does not bypass them or build a separate wakefulness minigame.

---

## Desired Player Experience

Caffeine becomes a strategic consumable:

* Drink it before the danger, not after you are already collapsing.
* Use coffee to cope and pills to push harder.
* Respect the tail.
* Respect sleep.
* Stack only when you are choosing tomorrow's problem on purpose.

The tradeoff should feel pharmacological, temporal, and believable.
